Why Sponsor CU SHPE?


On behalf of the Columbia University Chapter of the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers, we would like to invite you to become involved with our chapter community. As our mission statement indicates "SHPE changes lives by empowering the Hispanic community to realize its fullest potential and to impact the world through STEM awareness, access, support and development".

Since the establishment of the Columbia University Chapter, we have focused on developing young and aspiring engineers into successful professionals around the world. Our corporate sponsorship packet outlines chapter budget and company sponsorship information for the academic school year and is meant to inform you about the various ways in which your company can become more involved with the SHPE-CU community.

CU-SHPE has numerous opportunities for corporate involvement and sponsorship during the 2015-2016 academic year. Our events include professional conferences, both at the national and regional levels, outreach programs within our community, and various events to develop personal and professional leadership skills for our members
